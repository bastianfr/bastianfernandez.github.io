Visual Hierarchy

Target

target.com

Target is a great example of this priciple. As we can see on their main page they have the 30% off showing bigger than anything else to help people see the sale. Next they have the item on sale. We can see how the most important part is bigger than the details.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

google.com

Google's website uses a lot of negative spacw which makes the google icon stand out even more. Since Google is a search engine it does not have a lot of things that will distract the user.

PARC: Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Since Apple has a variety of products to offer, they use the colors white and black to display and separate those products. Since these colors are opposite they create an obvious contrast.

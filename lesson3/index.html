Site purpose

The main purpose of Salio El Sol is to provide minute by minute updates on the weather. Salio El Sol allows you to select multiple cities in order to stay up to date on the weather in multiple areas. Our goal is to make sure our users have the ability to acquire the knowledge they need to be prepared for anything that comes their way. Chances of snow, rain, wind, hail, and more are all amongst the weather updates we pay special care to inform.
